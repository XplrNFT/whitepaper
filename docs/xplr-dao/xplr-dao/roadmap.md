# XPLR Roadmap

![XPLR NFT Platform](../../assets/images/xplr-mascot.jpg)

## 🚀 Our Vision for the Future

XPLR is committed to building the world's most comprehensive NFT trading platform. Our roadmap outlines our strategic initiatives to revolutionize the NFT market through innovation, community engagement, and sustainable growth.

---

## 📅 Development Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation & Launch 🎯

**Start NFT Trading Market**
- Launch core trading functionality
- Implement pooling algorithm
- Enable peer-to-peer NFT trading
- Deploy initial smart contracts

**Status**: ✅ In Progress

---

### Phase 2: Expansion & Multi-Chain 🌍

**Expand Multi-Chain Plan**
- Deploy on Ethereum
- Deploy on Polygon
- Deploy on BNB Chain
- Deploy on Arbitrum
- Deploy on Optimism
- Cross-chain bridge integration

**Benefits**:
- Increased accessibility
- Lower transaction costs
- Higher liquidity across chains
- Better user experience

**Status**: 🔄 Planned

---

### Phase 3: Governance & Community 🏛️

**Launch XPLR DAO Governance Program**
- Community voting on platform decisions
- Proposal submission system
- Treasury management
- Stakeholder rewards
- Decentralized decision-making

**Key Features**:
- Democratic governance
- Transparent processes
- Community-driven development
- Fair voting mechanisms

**Status**: 🔄 Planned

---

### Phase 4: Economic Model & Tokenomics 💰

**Launch Economic Model**
- XPLR token distribution
- Reward mechanisms
- Fee structure optimization
- Sustainability measures
- Long-term value creation

**Components**:
- Trading rewards (1.8% - 4.1%)
- Referral bonuses (up to 1.3%)
- Staking incentives
- Governance rewards

**Status**: 🔄 Planned

---

### Phase 5: Staking & Yield 📈

**Start Token Staking**
- XPLR token staking pools
- Attractive APY rates
- Flexible lock periods
- Automated reward distribution
- Enhanced user benefits

**Staking Benefits**:
- Passive income generation
- Governance participation rights
- Platform fee discounts
- Exclusive features access

**Status**: 🔄 Planned

**Start NFT Staking**
- NFT collection staking
- Yield generation from NFTs
- Rarity-based rewards
- Community-driven collections
- Long-term value appreciation

**Staking Features**:
- Earn while holding
- No impermanent loss
- Flexible unstaking
- Bonus multipliers

**Status**: 🔄 Planned
