# User Identity

The platform has six user levels (LV1 - LV6). Users start at LV1 and upgrade by participating in trading and promoting the platform to enjoy more benefits.

| User Level | Required Points | Valid A-Level Referrals | Valid B and C-Level Referrals | Remarks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| LV1 | / | / | / | Deposit at least $50 to activate LV1. |
| LV2 | 50 | 2 | 3 | Team Size: 5 |
| LV3 | 100 | 5 | 15 | Team Size: 20 |
| LV4 | 200 | 10 | 30 | Team Size: 40 |
| LV5 | 300 | 20 | 60 | Team Size: 80 |
| LV6 | 500 | 30 | 150 | Team Size: 180 |

Different user levels have different daily reservation quotas and auction price ranges.

| User Level | LV1 | LV2 | LV3 | LV4 | LV5 | LV6 |
